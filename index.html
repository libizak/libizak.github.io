About me

Who I am

Hi, my name is Libi Zak and I am an 2D and 3D artist and animator currently studying at the Bezalel Academy of Arts and Design in Jerusalem.

I was born and raised in Russia. I studied and worked in Israel, and am relocating to the USA in the nearest future.

Throughout my studies, I have gained a strong foundation in various techniques and software programs specified in the cinematic arts, 3D and 2D animation. I am particularly interested in telling stories, developing art for unique projects and bring characters to life.

I am always looking for new opportunities to grow and develop as an artist, and I am excited to see where my career will take me in the future. Among my projects: freelance animation for academic content, participation in documentary feature film as a 2D animator, a variety of personal short animation films and other projects. Thank you for visiting my portfolio website. I hope you enjoy viewing my work as much as I enjoyed creating it.

What I do

2D animation, 3D animation based on MAYA software, 3D art and modeling, mixed technics and filming assistance. Remote part time / freelance position. e projects and bring characters to life.
